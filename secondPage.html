My Travels in Europe!

Italy!

I visited Rome, Florence, Pisa, and Venice. By visiting these places, I got insight into Italian Culture, History, and Food. I got to visit historic monuments like the Leaning Tower of Pisa, the Panthenon, and the Duomo, all while having a constant cup of gelato in hand.

Greece!

I got to visit Greece this past summer. During my time in the country, I got to visit Athens, Meteora, and Santorini. Through my travels, I was able to meet the kindest souls. However, since my family and I went during peak tourist season, the streets were packed, and cruise ships were bringing 45,000 tourists to the islands everyday. Thus, when you travel there, I reccomend going during the shoulder or off season.

My Travels in Asia!

India!

I have been traveling to India since I was young. However, to me, it is not a place of tourist attractions, but an opportunity to see family that I do not get to see often. When I go visit, I get to see my grandparents as well as temples that have been around for centuries. Additionally, I get to have some of the most delcious food that the world has to offer.

Cambodia!

I have also gotten to visit Ankhor Watt in Cambodia. While there, I got to see ancient temples, and night markets. The experience was amazing and hopefully get to go back in the future.

Recommendations!

  1. Have a structured plan of what you want to see

  2. Prior research on the country

  3. Find out the best places to eat
